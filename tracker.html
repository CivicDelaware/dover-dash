⚠️ Primary registration deadline: Aug 22 — You must be registered as a Democrat or Republican to vote in the Sept 15 primary. Unaffiliated/independent voters cannot vote in party primaries under current Delaware law.

Already registered as unaffiliated? The deadline to change party affiliation was May 29 — it has passed. You cannot switch parties in time for the primary. New to the rolls? You can still register as D or R by Aug 22.
