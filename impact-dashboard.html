IMPACT DASHBOARD · METHODOLOGY
How Scoring Works
Each bill gets a Fiscal Risk Score (0–100) from its legal category's baseline weight, keyword signals in its text (e.g. "appropriation," "tax rate," "bond"), and its number of amendments. That score maps to a Dover Dash Risk Grade, a modeled scale using rating-style lettering (Aaa = minimal risk → Caa = severe risk).
Aaa/Aa · Minimal–Low A · Low-Moderate Baa · Moderate Ba · Elevated B/Caa · High–Severe
